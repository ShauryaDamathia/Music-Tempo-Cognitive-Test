Practice Task

Research study conducted by Meher Damathia (MSc. Clinical Psychology), Christ University

Instructions:

You will see five letters on the screen at a time.

Your task is to respond to the letter in the middle (the target letter), while ignoring the others.

Please respond as quickly and accurately as possible; if you do not respond within 3 seconds, your answer will be marked as incorrect.


Key responses:

Press A if the middle letter is X or C.

Press L if the middle letter is V or B.


Example: If the screen shows XXCXX, you should press A, because the middle letter is C.

These keys are used because they are positioned to the left (A) and right (L) sides of the keyboard.


Important notes:

  • Use only a laptop or desktop.
  • Headphones are required to ensure accurate sound delivery during the study.
  • Sit in a quiet environment with minimal distractions.
  • Respond as quickly and accurately as you can.
  • There will be a short practice round before the actual task begins.
